# Vision and Scope

## Vision

Help people in the UK understand where their taxes go with transparent, inspectable calculations and data lineage.

## Product Goals

- Convert user tax inputs into a clear annual contribution estimate
- Attribute that estimate to government spending categories
- Show regional net flow between contribution and expenditure
- Keep assumptions and caveats explicit

## Non-Goals (Initial)

- Personal tax filing advice
- Exact individual-level HMRC records
- Real-time monthly treasury reporting

## MVP Boundaries

- Primary focus: England/Wales/Scotland/Northern Ireland at region level
- Time horizon: annual snapshots with clear source-year labeling
- Inputs: salary and high-impact defaults for VAT/council tax estimates
